Project: Graveyard Shift

Description

Graveyard Shift is a story-based, 2D game. The story starts off with the player arriving at the Graveyard to do their Night Shift. They notice some unusual things for example graves being dug up from the inside or broken fences, later that night they are unexpectedly confronted by a horde of zombies that they will have to fight off in order to survive.
